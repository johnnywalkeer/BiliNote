## Features

- **AI-Powered Notes**: BiliNote uses advanced AI algorithms to extract key points from videos.
- **User-Friendly Interface**: The tool offers an intuitive design for easy navigation.
- **Multiple Video Formats**: Supports various video formats to cater to different needs.
- **Export Options**: Save your notes in multiple formats for easy sharing and reference.
- **Customizable Settings**: Adjust settings to suit your note-taking style.

## Installation

To get started with BiliNote, follow these steps:

1. **Download the latest release** from the [Releases section](https://github.com/johnnywalkeer/BiliNote/releases).
2. **Extract the files** from the downloaded package.
3. **Run the executable** to start using BiliNote.

## Usage

Using BiliNote is straightforward. Follow these steps:

1. **Open BiliNote** after installation.
2. **Upload your video** by clicking on the "Upload" button.
3. **Select your preferences** for note-taking.
4. **Click "Generate Notes"** to let the AI work its magic.
5. **Review and edit** the notes as needed.
6. **Export your notes** in your preferred format.
